GRANT CARDONE

American entrepreneur, New York Times Best Selling Author, Speaker, Motivator

Cardone is an internationally-renowned speaker on sales, leadership, real-estate investing, entrepreneurship and finance whose passion is to help people grow their sales by customizing their sales processes to be more effective.

His journey started from being a poor kid from Louisiana, to barely surviving as a drug addict, to then making the most of his situation to rise to superstardom as one of the top motivational leaders internationally.
